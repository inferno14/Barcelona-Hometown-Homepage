Top three activities to do in Barcelona

Be awestruck by the Sagrada Familia

You couldn’t come to Barcelona without marvelling at its contrasting facades, sculpted details and stained glass.

Soak up best views at Gaudi’s Park Guell

It is another big-hitting Gaudi attraction, Park Guell is a glorious colourfully-tiled park with sculptures, gardens, the lot.

Chill out in the sun at Barcelona’s beaches

You may getting in touch with nature, or you want to play volleyball, swim in the Med or do any activities with the word ‘surf’ in them.
